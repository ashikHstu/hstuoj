About Us

Our Idea

Competitive programming is a vital part in our study now a days. So it is essential to study & practice regularly to get the concept clear in programming. An online judge can help us in this case. HSTU Coding Lab is a platform, where competitive programmers can solve problem and submit them. It also contain custom code editor, where a programmer can do custom code, and get their output. By using our platform, A programmer can use IDE online. As a result, they don't need to install additional IDE in their computer locally. Our Platform helps user to get online IDE experience.
